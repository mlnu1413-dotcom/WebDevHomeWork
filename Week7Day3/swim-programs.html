Summer Swim Camps

Building Swimming Skills All Summer Long

Weekly camp sessions help swimmers improve stroke technique, breathing, starts, turns, and speed in a fun, active environment.

Summer Swim Camps

If your child is between the ages of 5 to 14 and can swim two lengths across a 25-yard pool unassisted, consider enrolling them in our summer swim camps. Discounts are available for students who enroll in three sessions or more.

During the day, students work with highly qualified instructors on technique, breathing, starts, turns, and speed work. Camps also include a snack, beverage, and swim games during the final portion of the day.
