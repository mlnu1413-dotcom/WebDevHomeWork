Which Lesson is Best?

Our Lesson Types

Choose the swim lesson format that matches your swimmer’s age, confidence, and learning style.

Private Swim Lessons

Our private swim lessons provide one-on-one instruction for students ages three months through adult. Private swim lessons are the best option for accelerated learning.

From the basics of putting your face into the water to refining stroke technique, private lessons offer the highest rate of progress.

Semi-Private Swim Lessons

Our semi-private swim lessons offer students ages 2.5 to 13 the opportunity to interact with another student of a similar age and ability while still receiving personal attention.

This format is a cost-effective option and gives swimmers the opportunity to observe one another during the lesson.

Parent & Me Lessons

Parent and Me lessons give parents the opportunity to be hands-on with their little one while our instructors facilitate the learning process.

Children ages 3 months through 3 years can participate in this fun, relaxed environment in the water.

Stroke Clinic

Stroke Clinics place emphasis on proper technique over speed and prepare students for swim team in a structured, group-setting environment.

Swimmers ages 5 and older who are level 8 or higher benefit from fast-paced sessions that reinforce strong foundations.

Adult Swim Lessons

It is never too late to learn how to swim. Our instructors tailor each adult lesson to your individual needs, from learning the basics to improving technique for exercise.

Lessons are geared for swimmers ages 13 and older, from beginners to advanced swimmers.
