Adult Lap Swimming

Adult lap swimming provides a full-body workout that targets the core, arms, back, and legs. It also gives swimmers access to various training methods, including strength, aerobic, and low-impact exercise.

If you are looking to improve your workout routine, stop by a Splashes Aquatics location to learn more about adult lap swimming and available weekly schedules.

Each lane will have up to two lap swimmers. Children under 14 must be accompanied by a parent and able to swim 200 yards on their own. Lanes are limited during shared pool usage.
